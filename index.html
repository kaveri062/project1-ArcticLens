Meet our experts

Samantha Collins

Samantha Collins is a seasoned photographer with a passion for capturing the beauty of the natural world. With over 15 years of experience in landscape and wildlife photography, Samantha has traveled to remote corners of the globe, documenting breathtaking landscapes and fascinating wildlife.

As an instructor, Samantha believes in the power of storytelling through images. Her workshops are designed to help aspiring photographers develop their artistic vision and technical skills while immersing themselves in nature's wonders. Samantha's teaching style is patient and supportive, and she enjoys sharing her knowledge and insights with students of all skill levels.

In addition to leading photography workshops, Samantha's work has been published in numerous magazines and exhibited in galleries worldwide. She is also a strong advocate for environmental conservation and uses her photography to raise awareness about the importance of preserving our planet's natural beauty.

Join Samantha on a photography workshop and learn to capture stunning images that evoke emotion and tell compelling stories.

Michael Rami

Michael Rami is a versatile photographer known for his expertise in portraiture and fashion photography. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for capturing the essence of his subjects, Michael has built a reputation for creating striking and memorable images.

With over a decade of experience in the industry, Michael has worked with top fashion magazines, renowned designers, and celebrities. He brings his wealth of knowledge and artistic approach to his photography workshops, where he helps participants unlock their creative potential and master the art of portraiture.

Michael's teaching style is energetic and dynamic, blending technical instruction with hands-on practice. He believes in fostering a collaborative learning environment, encouraging students to experiment and push the boundaries of their creativity. Through personalized feedback and guidance, Michael empowers photographers to develop their own unique style.

Join Michael on a photography workshop and discover the art of capturing captivating portraits that leave a lasting impression.
